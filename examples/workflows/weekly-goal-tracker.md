# Weekly Goal Tracker

A weekly check-in that compares what you said you'd do with what you actually did — then helps you adjust.

Create a workflow that runs every Sunday at 8pm. Look at my goals and priorities, then review what I actually did this week — messages I sent, PRs I merged, meetings I had, tasks I completed. For each goal, tell me honestly whether I made progress, what specifically moved it forward, and what got in the way. If a goal hasn't had any activity in two weeks, flag it. Give me wins to feel good about, and tell me what to prioritize Monday morning to get back on track.
