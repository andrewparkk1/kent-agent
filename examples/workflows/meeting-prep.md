# Meeting Prep

Checks your calendar daily, researches the people you're meeting, and creates private prep notes so you walk in with context.

Create a workflow that runs every day at 7am and checks my calendar for the week. For every meeting where I'm meeting a person, search the web for their background — who they are, what their company does, their role, anything public and relevant. Then look up context from my past messages, emails, meeting notes, and memories. Create a private calendar event at the same time and date as the meeting (only visible to me) with a description that has everything I need to know: their background, what their company does, what we last talked about, any open threads or things I said I'd follow up on, and anything relevant from recent emails or messages. Use bullet points so it's easy to scan quickly. No markdown formatting. Title it something like "Prep: [person's name]".
