# Calendar from Messages

Scans your iMessage, Signal, and Gmail for plans, deadlines, and commitments — then adds them to your calendar automatically.

Create a workflow that runs every 30 minutes and scans my recent messages from iMessage, Signal, and Gmail for anything that should be on my calendar. Look for plans people mention ("let's grab coffee Tuesday at 2"), deadlines ("this is due Friday"), reservations, bookings, bills due, and commitments I made ("I'll send that by Thursday"). Create calendar events or tasks for each one. Don't duplicate things that are already on my calendar. Include who mentioned it in the event description so I have context.
