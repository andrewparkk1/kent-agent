# Daily Goal Tracker

A quick end-of-day check on whether today moved you closer to your goals — and what stopped you if it didn't.

Create a workflow that runs every day at 9pm. Look at my goals and what I actually did today — messages, meetings, tasks completed, code pushed, emails sent. For each active goal, write a short paragraph covering what I did (or didn't do) that moved the needle, whether anything blocked me or pulled me off track, and how today's effort fits into the bigger picture. If a goal got zero attention, say why — was it deprioritized, or did something else eat the time? End with one specific thing I can do tomorrow to make progress on whatever's falling behind. Keep it honest and concise — this is a daily gut check, not a full review.
